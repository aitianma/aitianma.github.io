Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of Large Model Systems, Distributed AI, and Algorithm-System Co-design. I focus on bridging cutting-edge AI research with practical deployment challenges, spanning ultra-lightweight edge AI to large-scale distributed inference systems.

  • Distributed LLM inference & efficient training
  • Edge AI and ultra-lightweight models
  • Algorithm-system co-design for ML production
  • Time series forecasting architectures

Selected Projects

📊 Large-Scale IoT Optimization
